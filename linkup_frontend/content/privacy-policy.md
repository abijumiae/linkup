## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create or manage an account, we may collect:

- Name, username, and email address
- Phone number, if provided
- Password or authentication credentials (stored in hashed form)
- Profile picture, cover image, bio, interests, skills, country, and language
- Account type and privacy preferences
- Social login information, if you use a third-party sign-in method

### User-Generated Content

We collect content you create, upload, send, or share, including posts (“Sparks”), comments, messages, voice notes, images, videos, moments, group/event/job/market content, and support or moderation submissions.

Audio and video room streams may be processed in real time to deliver the Service. We do **not** store live audio or video streams unless a feature clearly states recording is enabled, or storage is required for safety, legal, or moderation purposes.

### Device and Technical Information

We may collect IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, app version, device identifiers, log data, crash reports, network performance data, approximate location from IP address, and access timestamps.

### Media and Device Permissions

With your permission, LinkUp may access camera, microphone, photos or gallery, storage or file picker, and notifications. You can manage permissions in device or browser settings. Disabling permissions may limit certain features.

### Safety and Moderation Data

We may collect user reports, block and privacy settings, moderation actions, suspicious activity signals, spam or abuse indicators, and account security logs.
